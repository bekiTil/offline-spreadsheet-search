# Spreadsheet Search Guide

## First time

1. Open the Spreadsheet Search link.
2. Click **Install Spreadsheet Search**.
3. Click **Install** when asked.
4. Open **Spreadsheet Search** from your computer.

## Everyday use

1. Open **Spreadsheet Search**.
2. Click **Add Files**.
3. Choose your spreadsheet files.
4. Choose the columns you want to search.
5. Click **Import Files**.
6. Click **Search**.

## Important

- Your spreadsheet files stay on this computer.
- Use the same computer and browser to keep your imported files.
- Do not clear app/site data unless you want to remove your imported files.
